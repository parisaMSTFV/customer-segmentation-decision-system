# Segment Definitions & Strategic Interpretation

Clusters were translated into actionable business segments to ensure interpretability and decision usability.

---

## 1. High Value Active

Profile:
- High order frequency
- High margin contribution
- Recent activity
- Strong engagement signals

Strategic Action:
- Prioritize retention
- Offer loyalty incentives
- Upsell high-margin products
- Early access campaigns

---

## 2. High Value At Risk

Profile:
- Historically high revenue
- Increasing recency (declining activity)
- Reduced engagement

Strategic Action:
- Targeted win-back campaigns
- Personalized retention offers
- Early churn detection monitoring

---

## 3. Mid Value / Growth Potential

Profile:
- Moderate purchase frequency
- Stable but not high margin
- Consistent engagement

Strategic Action:
- Cross-sell
- Incentivized upgrades
- Category expansion nudges

---

## 4. Engaged Non-Buyer

Profile:
- High browsing behavior
- Low purchase frequency
- Strong product interaction signals

Strategic Action:
- Conversion optimization
- First-purchase incentives
- Cart-based targeting

---

## 5. Low Engagement / Low Value

Profile:
- Low sessions
- Low purchase frequency
- High recency

Strategic Action:
- Low-cost automated campaigns
- Suppression from expensive paid channels
